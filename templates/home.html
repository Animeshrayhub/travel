Sacred & Scenic

Puri

🙏 The Sacred Beach City

Famous for the Jagannath Temple and its pristine golden beach. A blend of divine spirituality and coastal serenity.

📍 60 km from BBSR ⏱️ 2–3 Days Ideal

Chilika Lake

🦩 Asia's Largest Brackish Lake

Spot Irrawaddy dolphins, flamingos, and 160+ bird species on a magical boat safari through India's most celebrated lagoon.

📍 110 km from BBSR ⏱️ 1–2 Days Ideal

🏛️ Temple City of India

Over 700 ancient temples dot this vibrant capital city. Home to the majestic Lingaraj Temple and prehistoric Udayagiri caves.
